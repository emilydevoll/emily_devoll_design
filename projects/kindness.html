The Kindness Project

Mobile App, Web Design

The Kindness Project is a hypothetical organization that was created by one of the Iron Yard's UI Design alumnea. It's foundation is based upon the powerful belief in making a positive impact on the lives of others and is dedicated to providing resources and tools that encourage performing good deeds. Our assignment was to create a social media based mobile app platform that allowed it's user to tell their stories and promote positivity by challenging others to do random acts of kindness in their communities.

Research

The design direction we went in was based on the theme of uniting the world through helping others and putting the "human" back into humanity.

This past weekend we were given the opportunity to design an app of an organization or idea created by one of the design alumni. Not only was it our first experience (at least for me, others may have designed apps before) designing on a mobile app platform, but it was also our first time getting to create with another designer in the class, being that we were put into teams for the assignment.
