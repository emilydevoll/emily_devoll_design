I'm quirky. I'm Creative. I'm fun.

Austin-based UI/UX designer who strives to master the art of web design by harmonizing structure and clean lines with bold colors and creative flare.

I’m an upbeat gal with a passion for helping others and making the world a happier, more beautiful place. When I’m not losing myself in design and code, I love watching indie movies and going on wildlife adventures with my Mini Australian Shepherd, Atticus. I’m also a part-time commission artist, making custom photorealism portraits using graphite and occasionally watercolor.

Background

I received my education in Human Development and Family Science from Oklahoma State University, from which I then started my journey as a child care director in Dallas, TX. It wasn't until I discovered my love of art and the impressive knack I have for creating it that I decided to move to Austin. The second I got here I was instantly smitten by the amazing smell of Austin's super awesome startups and prominent tech companies and I knew that's where my next adventure would take me. Although my experience seems to consist of only 3 months from attending The Iron Yard's UI design program, I can assure you that the amount of knowledge I gained in such a short period of time will amaze you. I can't get enough of this stuff and I'm so stoked for what's to come.

Process

1. Research on current trends/competitors, create:

  • User Interviews
  • User Personas
  • Use Cases/Scenarios
  • SWOT Analysis

2. Theme Creation that's creative and unique using:

  • Wordlists
  • Style Tiles

3. Design Structure creation through making:

  • User Flows
  • Sketches
  • Wireframes
  • Paper Prototypes

4. Mockups using illustrator to help better visualize the final product

5. Code using:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • SCSS/SASS
  • jQuery
