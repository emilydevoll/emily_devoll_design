I'm quirky. I'm Creative. I'm fun.

Austin-based UI/UX designer who strives to master the art of web design by harmonizing structure and clean lines with bold colors and creative flare.

I’m an upbeat gal with a passion for helping others and making the world a happier, more beautiful place. When I’m not losing myself in design and code, I love watching indie movies and going on wildlife adventures with my Mini Australian Shepherd, Atticus. I’m also a part-time commission artist, making custom photorealism portraits using graphite and occasionally watercolor.

I started my journey as a director of an elementary school's child care program in Dallas, TX, but it was through my love of art and design that led me to Austin. I've been here for almost 2 years and it's nice to finally live in an atmosphere that's driven by creativity, and to be accepted and surrounded by like-minded people.

It's been one crazy ride so far, but I'm loving every second of it. I've never been more excited than I am right now to see what's in store as I start my next chapter in life: UI Design.
