I'm quirky. I'm Creative. I'm fun.

Austin-based designer who strives to master the art of mobile app and web design by harmonizing structure and clean lines with a creative flare. I love establishing what users need and expect, testing and validating those hypotheses, and then getting to create dope designs that make those visions come alive.

I’m an art junkie, independent films fanatic, and lover of Australian Shepherds with a heart for making the world a happier, easier, and more beautiful place.

Background

I received my education in Human Development and Family Science from Oklahoma State University, from which I became a child care director in Dallas, TX. It was my art that brought me to Austin and I was instantly smitten by the heavily tech powered atmosphere here. I knew that the world of design was where my next adventure would take me.

I completed The Iron Yard’s UI Design bootcamp program in 2015 and after that my fate as a designer was sealed.

Process

1. PRODUCT STRATEGY and information architecture creation:

  • Assessing cognitive psychology
  • Designating key features and areas of focus
  • Establishing navigation and hierarchy
  • Creating hypotheses to be tested

2. USER RESEARCH engagement:

  • Recruiting testing participants
  • Conducting user interviews
  • Analyzing data through experience maps
  • Creating rich user stories based on findings

3. VISUAL DESIGNS creation:

  • Wireframes and mockups
  • Branding and logos
  • Illustrations and graphics
  • Prototypes
