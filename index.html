About Me

I'm quirky. I'm creative. I'm fun.

Austin based designer who strives to master the art of web design by harmonizing structure and clean lines with bold colors and creative flare.

Part-time photorealism portrait artist, independent film fanatic, lover of Australian Shepherds, with a passion for helping others.

I started my journey as a director of an elementary school's after school child care program in Dallas, TX, but it was through my adventures in art that led me to Austin. I've been here for 2 years and its nice to finally live in a place where I am surrounded by fellow weirdos. It's been one crazy ride, but I've loved every second of it.
